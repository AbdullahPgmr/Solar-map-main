About the Project

Welcome to the Heliocentric Star Map project, where we delve into the wonders of our solar system through a captivating visual journey. Our project aims to showcase the intricate orbits and relationships of celestial bodies orbiting the Sun, offering a unique perspective on our cosmic neighborhood. Through meticulous data representation and innovative visualization techniques, we aim to provide a comprehensive and educational experience. Discover the stories behind planetary discoveries, the dynamics of orbits, and the significance of celestial phenomena in shaping our understanding of the universe. Join us as we explore the beauty and complexity of the cosmos, blending scientific accuracy with artistic interpretation to create a visually stunning and intellectually enriching resource. Whether you're a student, an astronomy enthusiast, or simply curious about the universe, our project invites you to embark on a journey of discovery and wonder.
